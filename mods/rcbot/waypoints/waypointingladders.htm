Waypointing Ladders:

  1. Create a waypoint at the bottom of the ladder
  2. Create a waypoint at the very top of the ladder on the ledge where the bot should walk on after leaving the ladder. If there is no ledge then put no-clip ON (See commands readme.) and then put a waypoint at this point inside the ladder. This is so that the bot knows what direction to look while climbing.
  3. If the last waypoint you made is not a "ladder waypoint" then use the waypoint menu to give it the ladder type.
  4. Make a normal waypoint at the very bottom of the ladder near the first ladder waypoint and give it a path to the ladder waypoint. Make sure the path to the ladder waypoint is facing the direction you want the bot to look when climbing the ladder.
  5. Make another normal waypoint nearby the last waypoint and make the only path point towards the last waypoint you made.
